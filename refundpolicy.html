Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

General Refund Policy

At Vision Infotech Limited, we are committed to providing high-quality digital solutions and services. We understand that circumstances may arise where you may need to request a refund. This policy outlines our refund terms and conditions.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Service cancellations requested within 14 days of the initial order may be eligible for a refund
  • Custom development projects are generally non-refundable once work has commenced
  • Monthly subscription services can be cancelled at any time with no further charges
  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after approval

Non-Refundable Services

  • Domain name registrations and renewals
  • Third-party software licenses
  • Completed design work and deliverables
  • Services rendered more than 30 days ago

Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team at info@visioninfotech.com with your order details and reason for the refund request. We will review each request on a case-by-case basis.

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds based on the work completed and resources utilized. The amount will be determined by our team after reviewing the project status.
